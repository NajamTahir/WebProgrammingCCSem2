Plot

Breaking Bad follows the story of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, and his former student, Jesse Pinkman. As Walter delves deeper into the drug trade, he faces numerous ethical dilemmas and increasingly dangerous situations.

Cast & Crew

Main Cast: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul, Anna Gunn

Creator: Vince Gilligan
